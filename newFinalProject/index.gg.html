A Memo from the GM

Dear Team,

As we dive into the exciting realm of the NBA, here are some captivating facts to enrich our understanding and spark enthusiasm:

Global Fandom: The NBA boasts a massive global fanbase, with broadcasts available in over 200 countries and territories.

It's not just a sport; it's a worldwide cultural phenomenon.

Tall Tales: The average height of an NBA player is around 6 feet 7 inches (200 cm). To put it into perspective, that's several inches

taller than the average height in most countries.

Record-Breaking Scoring: The highest-scoring game in NBA history occurred on December 13, 1983, between the Detroit Pistons and

the Denver Nuggets, with a final score of 186-184 in triple overtime!

Team Dominance: The Boston Celtics hold the record for the most NBA championships, boasting a staggering 17 titles, followed closely by

the Los Angeles Lakers with 17 championships.
